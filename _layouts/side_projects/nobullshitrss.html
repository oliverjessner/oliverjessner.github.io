Dashboard

Scan fast. Filter faster.

  • Chronological feeds. The latest is actually the latest.
  • Define your own topics and auto-classify matching articles.
  • Filter dashboard articles by topic to focus faster.
  • Select any word to instantly search for related content.
  • Lightning filtering with tags, lists, and smart buckets.
Digest

Briefings on your cadence.

  • Run digest briefings daily, weekly, or monthly.
  • Add digest articles directly to your reading lists.
  • Tune digest runs with blocked words and excluded sources.
  • Read articles inside the in-app browser without breaking flow.
  • Offline-friendly reading lists.
